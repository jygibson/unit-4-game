diamond collector

you will be given a random number at the beginning of the game.
there are four diamonds to choose from. by clicking on a diamond, you will add a specific amount of points to your total.
you win the game by matching your points to the random number. you lose the game if your total number goes over the random number
the value of each diamond is hidden from you until you click on it.
each time the game starts, the random number and diamond values will change.
